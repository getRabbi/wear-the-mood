# Google Play screenshots — Wear The Mood (`com.fashionos.app`)

Screenshot plan for the Play listing (§3 of `PLAY_STORE_CHECKLIST.md`), matched to
`STORE_LISTING.md` and the current app. Docs only — no code changes.

**Rules for the copy/visuals:** no claims of a *perfect fit*, AR, medical accuracy,
or *guaranteed photorealism*. Keep captions aspirational but honest. Never show a
real person's private photos, real emails, or other private user data — use demo /
seed content.

---

## Design specs (apply to every screenshot)
- **Size:** 1080 × 1920 px, portrait PNG/JPG (Play accepts 320–3840 px per side; 1080×1920 is the sweet spot).
- **Layout:** caption band in the **top ~22%**, the app screen below it. Keep all text inside a safe area — **≥120 px from the top**, **≥160 px from the bottom** (Play overlaps the bottom with UI).
- **Caption:** large + readable — ~60–72 px bold display (Fraunces-style) for the headline; one short line, max two. High contrast on the plum band.
- **Brand style (dark luxury):** deep **plum/aubergine** background, **lavender** + **pink** accents, soft glow; consistent across all 8 so they read as a set.
- **Consistency:** same caption position, type, and accent on every frame. Optional thin device frame — but be consistent (all framed or all full-bleed).
- **Privacy:** demo avatars/models only (no founder selfies); fake display names; blur or replace any real handles, emails, or faces you don't have rights to.

---

## The screenshots (suggested Play Console order = the order below)

### 1. AI try-on result — THE HOOK  *(real screen · staged/demo data)*
- **App area:** MoodMirror / Try-On result (ideally a **before → after** split: the plain photo vs the styled look).
- **Visible:** a clean demo model wearing a try-on outfit; the FREE badge; a tasteful result card.
- **Caption:** `See any look on you — before you buy`
- **Why it converts:** the core promise in one image; the "wow" that earns the install. First impression = highest weight.

### 2. Try-On Studio in action  *(real screen · demo garments)*
- **App area:** the 2D Outfit Stack editor (garments layered on the body, the tool bar).
- **Visible:** a top + bottom + jacket composed on a body, layer thumbnails, the "Done" CTA.
- **Caption:** `Style full outfits on yourself in seconds`
- **Why it converts:** shows it's interactive and easy — not just a single canned render.

### 3. Digital closet  *(real screen · curated demo wardrobe)*
- **App area:** Wardrobe / Closet grid (the drawer view).
- **Visible:** a full, tidy grid of clothing cutouts — never empty.
- **Caption:** `Your whole wardrobe, digitized`
- **Why it converts:** signals daily utility + a data moat; appeals to organizers, not just shoppers.

### 4. Daily AI stylist  *(real screen · demo data)*
- **App area:** Home "Today" / daily stylist suggestion.
- **Visible:** "What to wear today" outfit suggestion, weather context, the daily guide.
- **Caption:** `What to wear today — styled for you`
- **Why it converts:** the habit loop / reason to open it every morning → retention story for store browsers.

### 5. Style community feed  *(real screen · demo posts)*
- **App area:** Community feed (outfit-of-the-day cards).
- **Visible:** a few demo OOTD posts with likes/comments.
- **Caption:** `Share your look. Find your style tribe`
- **Why it converts:** social proof + belonging; shows the app is alive, not a solo tool.

### 6. Challenges & Style leaderboard  *(real screen · demo names)*
- **App area:** Style leaderboard / challenges.
- **Visible:** the monthly leaderboard with demo ranks + a challenge entry.
- **Caption:** `Join challenges. Top the Style leaderboard`
- **Why it converts:** gamification = engagement; differentiates from plain try-on apps.

### 7. Free clothes — Community giveaways  *(real screen · demo listings)*
- **App area:** Community → Giveaway grid.
- **Visible:** the give-it-forward banner + a grid of free pieces.
- **Caption:** `Give & claim clothes — for free`
- **Why it converts:** a unique, generous hook with no paywall; strong word-of-mouth angle.

### 8. Premium — HD AI try-on  *(real screen OR staged · demo data)*
- **App area:** AI Realistic HD result or the paywall value screen.
- **Visible:** a higher-fidelity try-on result + "HD / unlimited looks" value points. **Do not** caption it "guaranteed photorealistic."
- **Caption:** `Upgrade to HD AI try-on & unlimited looks`
- **Why it converts:** sets up monetization honestly while the free value carries the install.

> If you ship **6** instead of 8, keep **1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 7** (drop leaderboard + premium).

## Capture source: real vs staged
- **Captured from the real app (with demo/seed data):** all 8 — they should be genuine app screens so they match what users get (Play penalizes misleading screenshots).
- **Must use staged/demo content (not real users):** #1, #5, #6, #7, #8 — anything showing people, names, or community posts. Seed a demo account + demo wardrobe/community so nothing private appears.
- **Pure UI, low risk:** #2, #3, #4 — still use demo garments, not personal photos.
- The **caption band** is added in design software (Figma/Canva) over the captured screen; only the screen itself comes from the device.
