Fashion OS is a virtual try-on and style community. To keep everyone safe, you
agree **not** to upload, generate, post, or share content that:

## Strictly prohibited

- **Depicts a real person without their consent**, including using anyone's photo
  but your own for avatar/try-on. Upload images of **yourself only**.
- Is **sexual, nude, or pornographic**, or sexualizes the try-on of any person.
- **Involves minors** in any way, or depicts anyone under 18.
- Is non-consensual intimate imagery, "deepfake"-style impersonation, or designed
  to harass, defame, threaten, or impersonate someone.
- Is hateful, harassing, or discriminatory toward people based on protected
  characteristics.
- Promotes violence, self-harm, terrorism, or illegal activity.
- Infringes intellectual-property or privacy rights you don't hold.
- Contains malware, spam, scams, or attempts to break or abuse the service.

## How we enforce

- We **moderate try-on inputs** before processing and **screen posts and
  comments** before they appear publicly. Violating content is blocked.
- You can **report** posts, comments, or users, and **block** users. Reports are
  reviewed and acted on.
- We may remove content, limit features, and **suspend or terminate accounts**
  that violate this policy, and report unlawful content to authorities where
  required.
