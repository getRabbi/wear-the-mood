The idea

What is Wear The Mood?

Wear The Mood is a fashion-tech mobile app designed to help you build your personal fashion system — organize your wardrobe, create outfits, explore inspiration, save favorite looks, share style ideas with the community, and use AI try-on to visualize fashion ideas before wearing them.

Visualize outfits

See ideas come to life before you commit to a look.

Organize wardrobe

Build looks

Explore inspiration

Save favorites

Bookmark the outfits and ideas you love.

Share style

Post your looks and inspire a creative community.
